Union By Thirds

For the purpose of analysis, the 6 input meta-networks were divided into three periods: Beginning, Middle, End.

The meta-networks for each period were combined into a single meta-network for the report analyses. An Overall period was also created that combined all of the meta-networks. How the meta-networks were divided into the periods is explained below.

Periods determined by the order of the input meta-networks:

Period Meta-networks Description
Beginning 2 first input meta-networks
Middle 2 middle meta-networks
End 2 final meta-networks
Overall 6 all meta-networks
